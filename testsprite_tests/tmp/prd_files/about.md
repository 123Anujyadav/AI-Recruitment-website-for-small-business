# About TalentAI

**TalentAI** is a cutting-edge, AI-powered job matching platform designed to bridge the gap between top-tier talent and forward-thinking employers. By leveraging advanced machine learning and semantic analysis, TalentAI streamlines the recruitment process, ensuring the right people find the right opportunities faster than ever before.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### For Candidates
*   **Intelligent Profile Building:** Create a comprehensive professional profile highlighting your skills, experience, and preferences.
*   **Smart Job Matching:** Receive job recommendations tailored to your unique skill set and location.
*   **AI Salary Assessment:** Get real-time, AI-driven insights into market-standard salaries for specific roles based on your profile.
*   **Personalized Learning Paths:** Identify skill gaps and receive custom-generated roadmaps to help you qualify for your dream job.
*   **Seamless Applications:** Apply to jobs with a single click and track your application status in real-time.

### For Employers
*   **Effortless Job Posting:** Create detailed job listings with specific skill requirements and location preferences.
*   **Automated Applicant Ranking:** View candidates ranked by a sophisticated "Match Score" that considers skill overlap and geographical proximity.
*   **Candidate Management:** Efficiently review, shortlist, or decline applicants through a streamlined company dashboard.
*   **Data-Driven Hiring:** Make informed hiring decisions backed by transparent matching metrics.

---

## 🧠 The TalentAI Intelligence Engine

At the core of TalentAI is a multi-layered intelligence system:

### 1. The Matching Algorithm
Our proprietary algorithm calculates a **Match Score (0-100)** for every application:
*   **Skill Synergy (70%):** A deep analysis of technical and soft skill overlap between candidate profiles and job requirements.
*   **Geographic Optimization (30%):** A location-based bonus that prioritizes local talent, reducing relocation friction and improving retention.

### 2. Google Gemini Integration
TalentAI integrates directly with **Google Gemini 1.5 Flash** to provide high-level career guidance:
*   **Market Analysis:** Analyzes current industry trends to provide accurate salary expectations.
*   **Skill-Gap Analysis:** Dynamically generates educational roadmaps, suggesting specific topics and resources to help candidates level up.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

TalentAI is built on a robust and modern stack designed for performance and scalability:
*   **Backend:** Python & Django REST Framework (DRF)
*   **Frontend:** Modern Vanilla JavaScript (Single Page Application architecture)
*   **AI:** Google Gemini API
*   **Database:** PostgreSQL
*   **Styling:** Premium Vanilla CSS for a smooth, responsive user experience.
